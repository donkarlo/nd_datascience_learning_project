\date{}
\author{Mohammad Rahmani}

\begin{document}
    \maketitle
    \cite{hinton-2006-reducing-the-dimensionality-of-data-with-neural-networks} proposed a deep autoencoder for nonlinear dimensionality reduction.
    The method first pretrains stacked restricted Boltzmann machines layer by layer, then unrolls them into an encoder--decoder network and fine-tunes the whole model with backpropagation.
    Their experiments showed that deep autoencoders can produce lower-dimensional codes with better reconstruction and visualization quality than PCA.
